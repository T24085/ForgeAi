Our History

The Abilene Downtown Antique Mall opened in 1995, transforming a historic building into a vibrant marketplace of pre‑owned treasures. Over 25 years, we’ve grown to host more than 15 dealers offering furniture, glassware, signs, and unique collectibles.

Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate heritage while creating a welcoming space for collectors, designers, and families to discover, share, and cherish timeless pieces.
