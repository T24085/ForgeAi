# Plan (The Hair Emporium - Starter)

## Summary
Starter tier website plan for The Hair Emporium – a full‑service salon and boutique. Focus on a clean, fast template with minimal copy, basic sections that showcase services, boutique, and contact information.

## Pages
- Home
- Services
- Boutique
- About
- Contact

## Sections
- Hero with tagline and call‑to‑action
- Services Overview (haircuts, nails, massages, tanning, eyelash extensions)
- Boutique Highlights (clothing, shoes, purses, jewelry)
- About the Salon (history, team, mission)
- Testimonials (short client quotes)
- Call‑to‑Action (book now)
- Contact Info (address, phone, hours, map)

## Style Notes
Modern, minimalistic design using a neutral color palette (soft grays, whites) accented with a vibrant brand color (e.g., teal or coral). Use clean sans‑serif typography, generous whitespace, and high‑resolution images. Ensure responsive layout for mobile and desktop.

## Assets Needed
- Logo (vector or PNG)
- High‑resolution images of salon interior and staff
- Images of boutique products
- Service icons or images
- Google Maps embed code
- Contact details (address, phone, hours)
- Social media icons (Facebook)
