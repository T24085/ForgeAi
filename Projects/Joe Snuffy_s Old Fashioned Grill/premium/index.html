Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take same-day reservations?

Yes. For parties under 6, same-day seating is usually available before 11:30 AM and after 1:30 PM.

Do you offer family-style takeout?

Yes. Family breakfast and lunch packs can be ordered by phone for pickup.

Are there vegetarian options?

We offer rotating vegetarian specials, veggie omelets, salads, and grilled sides daily.
