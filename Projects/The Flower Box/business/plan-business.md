# Plan (The Flower Box - Business)

## Summary
A comprehensive website plan for The Flower Box, featuring a clean, botanical-inspired design with enhanced navigation, a dedicated product catalog, an interactive gallery, a blog for floral tips, and a polished contact interface to boost branding and online presence.

## Pages
- Home
- About Us
- Products
- Gallery
- Blog
- Contact

## Sections
- Hero Banner
- Our Story
- Featured Products
- Seasonal Arrangements
- Gift Cards & Accessories
- Customer Testimonials
- Flower Care Tips
- Location & Hours
- Social Media Links
- Newsletter Sign-Up
- FAQ

## Style Notes
Modern, airy layout with a green‑and‑white color palette, serif typography for elegance, high‑resolution floral imagery, subtle animation on hover, responsive grid for mobile, and a custom logo integration with a botanical icon.
