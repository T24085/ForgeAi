# Plan (The Flower Box - Premium)

## Summary
Premium, visually rich e‑commerce website for The Flower Box featuring dynamic animations, deep product storytelling, SEO‑optimized content, lead capture forms, and optional integrations such as Shopify or WooCommerce, email marketing, and local SEO tools.

## Pages
- Home
- Shop
- About Us
- Services
- Custom Orders
- Blog
- Gallery
- Contact Us
- FAQ
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service

## Sections
- Hero with animated banner
- Featured collections carousel
- About story & values
- Service highlights (flowers, candles, gifts, decor)
- Custom order process with step‑by‑step guide
- Product detail pages with 360° view and video
- Blog carousel with latest posts
- Gallery with high‑resolution images & video reels
- Client testimonials slider
- Lead capture opt‑in (newsletter & discount)
- Map & store locator
- Contact form with map and business hours
- FAQ accordion
- Footer with links, social icons, privacy & terms

## Style Notes
Premium aesthetic using soft floral palettes (lavender, blush, sage) paired with crisp typography (serif for headings, sans‑serif for body). Subtle hover animations, scroll‑triggered parallax effects, and micro‑interactions on product cards. Fully responsive grid, dark mode option, and accessibility‑first design (WCAG 2.1 AA).

## Assets Needed
- High‑resolution product photos (front, back, close‑up, lifestyle)
- Lifestyle images of arrangements in home settings
- Short promotional videos (30‑60s) showcasing bouquet making
- Custom logo & favicon
- Social media icons (customized), "Shop Now" button graphics
- SEO keyword list & meta tag templates
- Copy for all pages (including SEO‑friendly headings)
- FAQ content
- Privacy & Terms text
- Email marketing template (newsletter, cart abandonment)
- API keys/credentials for optional integrations (Shopify/WooCommerce, Mailchimp, Google Maps)
