# Plan (The Flower Box - Starter)

## Summary
Starter tier website plan for The Flower Box: a fast, template-based site with minimal copy and essential sections to showcase products, shop location, and contact details.

## Pages
- Home
- Shop
- About
- Contact

## Sections
- Hero Banner
- Featured Products / Highlights
- About the Store
- Services & Offerings
- Customer Testimonials (optional placeholder)
- Shop Locations & Hours
- Contact Form & Map
- Social Media Links

## Style Notes
Use a clean, floral-themed template (e.g., Bootstrap or Shopify Simple). Colors: pastel pink/green palette with white background. Typography: sans-serif (e.g., Open Sans) for readability. Minimalist layout with generous white space, 2–3 grid columns for product grid. Hover effects on product images. Mobile-first responsive design. Simple navigation bar with logo on left, menu on right.

## Assets Needed
- Logo (vector or PNG, 200x200)
- Hero image of storefront or floral arrangement (1600x600)
- 3–5 product images (high-res, 1200x1200)
- Map image or embed placeholder for location
- Social media icons (SVG)
- Optional: small testimonial photo(s) if available
