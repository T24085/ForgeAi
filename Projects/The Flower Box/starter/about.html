About The Flower Box

Learn about our family‑owned shop and our passion for flowers.

Founded in 2026, The Flower Box brings fresh, locally sourced blooms to the heart of Abilene, KS. Our team crafts custom arrangements, candles, and gifts tailored to every occasion. We pride ourselves on sustainability, community involvement, and personalized customer service.

Visit our store at 421 N Spruce St or contact us for custom orders and floral design consultation.
