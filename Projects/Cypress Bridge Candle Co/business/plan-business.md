# Plan (Cypress Bridge Candle Co. - Business)

## Summary
A polished, brand‑focused website for Cypress Bridge Candle Co. featuring a modern layout, expanded content, and ecommerce capabilities to showcase hand‑poured candles and home décor gifts. The design will emphasize the company’s heritage, artisanal quality, and local presence, providing clear navigation, engaging visuals, and a seamless shopping experience.

## Style Notes
Branding: Warm, earthy palette (deep greens, muted golds, soft neutrals). Typography: Serif for headings (e.g., Playfair Display) and clean sans-serif body (e.g., Lato). Imagery: High‑resolution product shots with natural lighting; lifestyle images featuring the candles in home settings. Layout: Consistent grid system with generous whitespace, responsive design. Interaction: Subtle hover effects, smooth transitions, and micro‑animations for call‑to‑actions. Tone: Friendly, artisanal, community‑focused.
