# Plan (Cypress Bridge Candle Co. - Premium)

## Summary
Premium tier website plan for Cypress Bridge Candle Co. featuring high‑quality visuals, subtle animations, in‑depth content, robust SEO, lead capture, and optional integrations for e‑commerce, social, and analytics.

## Pages
- Home
- About Us
- Shop
- Product Detail
- Blog
- FAQs
- Contact
- Gift Registry
- Wholesale
- Terms & Privacy

## Sections
- Hero Banner (animated background, tagline, CTA)
- Featured Products Carousel
- Brand Story with parallax imagery
- Production Process Timeline
- Sustainability Commitment
- Customer Testimonials (video & text)
- Newsletter Signup with opt‑in
- Shop Category Grid with filter & sort
- Product Detail Layout (image zoom, video, spec, add to cart, upsell)
- Blog Posts Grid with categories & tags
- FAQs Accordion
- Contact Form + Map + Store Hours
- Gift Registry Builder
- Wholesale Application Form
- Footer (social links, navigation, copyright)

## Style Notes
Warm neutral palette (beige, amber, charcoal) with subtle gold accents. Use of high‑resolution product and lifestyle images, slow‑motion video backgrounds, parallax scrolling, micro‑interactions (hover fades, button press animations), and CSS grid for responsive layout. Typography: modern sans‑serif (e.g., Montserrat) for headings, serif (e.g., Playfair Display) for body text. Dark mode option. SEO‑optimized meta tags, schema markup, and structured data for products.

## Assets Needed
- High‑resolution product photos (front, back, close‑up, lifestyle)
- Lifestyle images of candles in home settings
- Brand logo (vector files)
- Short promotional video (30‑60s) for Hero section
- Customer testimonial videos & quotes
- Copy for brand story, production process, sustainability, FAQs, terms, privacy
- Product catalog (SKUs, pricing, descriptions, variations)
- Icon set for UI elements (cart, wishlist, filters)
- Micro‑interaction designs (SVGs or CSS snippets)
- SEO keyword list & meta description drafts
- Analytics tracking IDs (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, optional e‑commerce tracking)
- Email template for newsletter signup confirmation
- Favicon and Apple touch icons
