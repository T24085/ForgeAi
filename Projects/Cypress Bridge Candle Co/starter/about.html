About Cypress Bridge Candle Co.

Founded in 2001, our family‑owned candle shop in Abilene offers hand‑poured soy candles in a variety of scents. We use sustainably sourced wax and natural essential oils, ensuring each candle is both fragrant and eco‑friendly.

Our dedication to quality and community has earned us a loyal customer base, and we continue to expand our range to include seasonal holiday collections and gift sets.
