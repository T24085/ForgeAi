# Plan (Cypress Bridge Candle Co. - Starter)

## Summary
A quick, clean starter website for Cypress Bridge Candle Co., featuring a warm, minimal layout with essential pages and sections to showcase hand‑poured candles and gift items, plus easy contact access.

## Pages
- Home
- Shop
- About
- Contact

## Sections
- Hero banner with tagline and primary CTA
- Featured products carousel
- About us brief section
- Testimonials or customer quote
- Contact details & map
- Social media links

## Style Notes
Warm, earthy color palette (amber, soft browns, cream). Use high‑resolution candle photography, subtle animations, responsive typography. Keep layout uncluttered with generous whitespace and easy navigation.

## Assets Needed
- Company logo (vector or PNG)
- High‑resolution product photos (at least 4–6 key candles)
- Background image for hero banner (e.g., candlelit scene)
- Google Maps embed or map image for address
- Social media icons
- Optional: short video clip of candle pouring
