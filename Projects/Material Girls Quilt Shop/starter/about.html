About Material Girls Quilt Shop

Material Girls Quilt Shop is dedicated to providing quality quilting supplies and fabrics to our community in Abilene, KS.

Founded with passion for quilting, we strive to make quilting accessible and enjoyable for everyone, from beginners to experienced quilters.

Our shop offers a wide selection of fabrics, patterns, tools, and supplies to help you create beautiful quilts.
