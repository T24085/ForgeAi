# Plan (Material Girls Quilt Shop - Business)

## Summary
A polished, brand‑centric website for Material Girls Quilt Shop that showcases its quilting supplies and fabrics through a clean, responsive design. The site will feature a compelling hero, detailed product categories, educational blog posts, a gallery of finished quilts, a shop front (optional for future e‑commerce), and easy contact methods. The layout prioritizes usability, accessibility, and SEO, while reinforcing the shop’s craftsmanship aesthetic.

## Style Notes
Use a warm, earthy color palette (soft browns, muted blues, cream) with a primary accent of deep teal. Typography: Playfair Display for headings, Lora for body text. Grid‑based layout with generous whitespace. High‑resolution product images with hover zoom. Mobile‑first responsive design with accessible contrast ratios (WCAG AA). Subtle animation on CTA buttons and hero scroll. Footer includes quick links, newsletter sign‑up, and social icons. Consistent icon set for services and shop categories.
