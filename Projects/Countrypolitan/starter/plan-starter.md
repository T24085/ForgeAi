# Plan (Countrypolitan - Starter)

## Summary
Starter tier website plan for Countrypolitan: a quick, template‑based site featuring minimal copy and essential sections to showcase the antique, home & garden emporium.

## Pages
- Home
- About
- Shop Highlights
- Contact

## Sections
- Hero (store front image with tagline)
- About Us (short intro, mission, history)
- Featured Products (carousel of 4–6 key items)
- Shop Highlights (services & seasonal décor focus)
- Contact Information (address, phone, hours, map link)
- Social Media Links (Facebook feed or icon)

## Style Notes
Classic country vibe – earthy color palette (browns, muted greens, cream), serif type for headings, sans‑serif body text, subtle wood‑texture background, clean layout with generous whitespace.

## Assets Needed
- Store front image (hero)
- Interior photos (shop highlights)
- Product images (featured carousel)
- Logo or brand mark
- Map snippet or location icon
